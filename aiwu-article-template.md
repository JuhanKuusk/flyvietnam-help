# AIWU Article Template for Vietnam Visa Autoblogging

## Article Structure Template

### Title Format
```
[Primary Keyword]: [Engaging Hook] | [Year/Update]
```
Examples:
- "Vietnam Visa Urgent: Get Your Visa in 24 Hours | 2025 Guide"
- "Vietnam E-Visa for Indians: Complete Application Guide"

### Article Sections

```
## Introduction (100-150 words)
- Hook with problem/pain point
- Brief answer to main question
- What reader will learn

## [Main Section 1] (200-300 words)
- Core information
- Step-by-step if applicable
- Use H3 for subsections

## [Main Section 2] (200-300 words)
- Additional details
- Common questions addressed
- Practical tips

## [Main Section 3] (150-250 words)
- Supporting information
- Related topics
- Expert tips

## FAQ Section (3-5 questions)
- Schema markup ready
- Direct answers
- Related long-tail keywords

## Conclusion (100 words)
- Summary of key points
- Clear CTA
- Internal link to apply page
```

## Publishing Schedule

### Phase 1 (Days 1-7): Foundation Content
- 10 articles per domain = 50 total
- Focus: High-volume keywords
- Priority: Urgent/emergency keywords

### Phase 2 (Days 8-14): Expansion
- 15 articles per domain = 75 total
- Focus: Long-tail keywords
- Priority: City-specific, nationality-specific

### Phase 3 (Days 15-30): Scale
- 15 articles per domain = 75 total
- Focus: Question keywords, comparison keywords
- Priority: Low competition opportunities

## Total: ~200 articles in 30 days

## Quality Checks

Before publishing each article:
1. ✅ Unique content (not duplicate)
2. ✅ Correct year/pricing
3. ✅ Working internal links
4. ✅ Meta title < 60 chars
5. ✅ Meta description < 160 chars
6. ✅ Featured image added
7. ✅ Categories/tags assigned
8. ✅ Schema markup present
